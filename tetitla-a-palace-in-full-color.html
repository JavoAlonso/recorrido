This is one of the numerous housing complexes located around the ceremonial center of Teotihuacan. Access through the entrance is not direct because of a wall which obstructs the view to its interior. Yet, once inside, one is truck by its complicated housing system, though its different phases of construction left exposed, complicate ones clear understanding of the layout.

 

In its earliest stage this group consisted of two independent groups, which over time spread out until they merged into the single architectural unit visible today. There area is bordered by high walls, and narrow roads set in apart from other groups of buildings.

 

Most of its rooms are elaborately painted, its murals portraying different themes related to the cosmos and religion in ancient Teotihuacan.
